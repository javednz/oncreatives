branding FAQ

Your questions answered

What's included in a brand identity package?

A complete brand identity package typically includes logo design, color palette, typography, visual identity guidelines, brand style guide, and essential brand assets in various formats.

How long does brand identity design take?

Brand identity projects typically take 4 – 8 weeks, depending on project scope and complexity. Pitch decks usually require 1 – 2 weeks. Rebranding projects vary based on the extent of the transformation.

What's the difference between branding and graphic design?

Branding creates the strategic foundation and visual identity of your business, while graphic design applies that identity across specific materials and communications.

Do you provide brand strategy or just visual design?

Our branding services combine strategic thinking with visual design. We develop brand positioning, messaging frameworks, and visual identity systems.

Can you help with pitch deck content or just design?

We offer both pitch deck design services and pitch deck consulting, helping with content strategy, narrative development, and visual presentation.

Do you create design systems?

Yes, we create comprehensive design systems including component libraries, design tokens, style guides, and documentation for maintaining brand consistency.
